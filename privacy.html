Privacy Policy

This privacy policy has been created to assist individuals who are concerned about how their 'Personally Identifiable Information' (PII) is handled online. In the context of US privacy law and information security, PII refers to data that can be used alone or combined with other details to identify, contact, or locate a specific person, or to identify an individual within a certain context. Please review our privacy policy thoroughly to understand how we collect, use, protect, or manage your Personally Identifiable Information when you use our website.

What personal information do we collect from the people that visit our blog, website or app?

When registering on our site, you may be required to provide details such as your name, email address, mailing address, phone number, Social Security number, driver's license number, work history, number of moving violations, any DWI/DUI records, or other relevant information to enhance your experience.

When do we collect information?

We collect information from you when you fill out a form.

How do we use your information?

We may utilize the information collected from you when you register, subscribe to our newsletter, participate in a survey or marketing communication, browse the website, or engage with specific site features in the following ways:

• To manage and oversee contests, promotions, surveys, or other site activities.

• To send occasional emails about your order, as well as updates on other products and services.

• To follow up after interactions, such as live chat, email, or phone inquiries.

How do we protect your information?

Our website undergoes regular scans to detect security vulnerabilities and threats, ensuring a safe browsing experience. We utilize routine malware scanning and store personal information within secure networks, accessible only to authorized personnel with special access privileges, who are required to maintain confidentiality. Additionally, all sensitive and credit-related data you provide is encrypted using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology. To protect your personal information, we implement multiple security measures when users enter, submit, or access their data. For convenience, we may store credit card details for more than 60 days to streamline future orders and automate billing processes.
