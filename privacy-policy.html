Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Your privacy holds immense value to us at AS CARGO LLC As a result, we have designed this policy to help you understand how we gather, employ, communicate, and make use of your personal information. Here's an overview of our privacy policy.

As you interact with our website, we collect specific information about you.

Our website, like other commercial ones, uses a standard technology called "cookies" (refer to the explanation below) and server logs to gather information about the usage of our site. The data gathered through cookies and server logs may include the date and time of visits, pages viewed, browser and device information, geographical location, duration spent on our site, websites visited immediately before and after ours, as well as your IP address.

Mobile Texting / Privacy SMS

Upon becoming a customer through our platforms, you provide your phone number to us at AS CARGO LLC.

We may use your phone number to confirm orders, deliver ongoing training or communication, and to send text updates, promotions, videos, audios, or messages associated with AS CARGO LLC or any of its programs or brands. If you wish to opt-out of receiving future text messages, you can do so at any time by following the opt-out instructions in the text message. By signing up for our products or programs, you grant us permission to text you until you indicate otherwise. Rest assured, we never sell or rent your phone number and we communicate with you solely regarding relevant programs.

Use of Cookies

A cookie is a minuscule text document, which often carries an anonymous unique identifier. When you visit a website, that site's computer asks your computer for permission to store this file in a section of your hard drive specifically designated for cookies. Each website can send its own cookie to your browser if your browser's preferences permit it, but to safeguard your privacy, your browser only allows a website to access the cookies it has previously sent to you, not the cookies sent to you by other websites.

Third-Party Analytics and Advertising Providers

We might use third-party services provided by Google or Facebook for analytics, marketing, or remarketing services. These companies compile statistical reports that aid in analyzing how visitors use our website, enabling us to advertise services or products based on these reports. Additionally, these companies gather statistical data that permits us and other companies to participate in marketing campaigns on their platforms. Such advertising services may occur on or by Facebook, Facebook Pixel, DoubleClick, Google Analytics, Google search results pages, or on a site in the Google Display Network. These companies use pixels (small snippets of code) to serve ads to someone based on past visits to our website or other websites.

IP Addresses

Your computer uses IP addresses each time you connect to the Internet. Your IP address is a number utilized by computers on the network to identify your computer. IP addresses are automatically gathered by our web server as part of demographic and profile data known as “traffic data” so that data (such as the Web pages you request) can be sent to you.

Email Information

Should you choose to correspond with us via email, we may retain the content of your email messages along with your email address and our responses. We provide the same protections for these electronic communications that we employ in the maintenance of information received online, by mail, and by telephone. This also applies when you register for our website, sign up through any of our forms using your email address or make a purchase on this site. For more information, please see the email policies below.

How Do We Utilize the Personal Information Provided by You?

Generally speaking, we employ personal information for the administration of our business activities, customer service, and offering other items and services to our customers and prospective customers.

We do not acquire personally-identifying information about you when you visit our site unless you willingly provide such information to us. Such information will neither be sold nor transferred to unaffiliated third parties without user approval at the time of collection.

We may disclose information when legally compelled to do so; in other words, when we believe in good faith that the law mandates it or for the protection of our legal rights.

We will communicate and respond to email inquiries. When you interact with us through our website or email, we will communicate with you via your email address to provide information about AS CARGO LLC, our programs, investment inventory, upcoming educational opportunities, or with educational opportunities offered by companies we've partnered with.

We may conduct analysis or research about our website, business, or product offerings. Occasionally, we may reach out to you to participate in surveys or provide information so that we can enhance the quality of our inventory, our programs, and our business, or to ask you to review your experience working with AS CARGO LLC.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. Information sharing to subcontractors in support services, such as customer service, is permitted. All other use case categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties

Email Policies

At AS CARGO LLC, we are committed to safeguarding the confidentiality of your email address. We do not sell, rent, or lease our subscription lists to third parties, and we will not disclose your personal information to any third-party individual, government agency, or company unless we are legally bound to do so.

We will use your email address solely to provide timely information about our courses, properties, events, and other items relevant to AS CARGO LLC.

We will maintain the information you send via email in accordance with applicable federal law.

CAN-SPAM Compliance

In compliance with the CAN-SPAM Act, all emails sent from AS CARGO LLC will clearly state who the email is from and provide clear information on how to contact the sender. In addition, all email messages will contain concise information on how to remove yourself from our mailing list to ensure you receive no further email communication from us.

Choice/Opt-Out

Our site offers users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from AS CARGO LLC and our partners by reading the unsubscribe instructions located at the bottom of any email they receive from us at any time. Users who no longer wish to receive our newsletter or promotional materials may opt-out of receiving these communications by clicking on the unsubscribe link in the email.

Use of External Links

Our site may contain links to many other websites. We cannot guarantee the accuracy of information found at any linked site. Links to or from external sites not owned or controlled by AS CARGO LLC do not constitute an endorsement by AS CARGO LLC or any of its employees of the sponsors of these sites or the products or information presented therein.

By accessing our website, you agree to be bound by our privacy policy. You hereby consent to the collection, storage, and distribution of your data as described within this privacy policy. We reserve the right to amend this privacy policy at any time with or without notice.

Please ensure that you read this privacy policy each time you use our website, so you will be familiar with any changes that may be implemented by AS CARGO LLC. Your continued use of our website will signify your acceptance of the changes to our privacy policy.

If you have questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please feel free to get in touch with us.
